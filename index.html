Tacticspedia

About Me Hi, my name is Zahi Ahmed. I have a passion for deckbuilding games like Teamfight Tactics, so I decided to make an encyclopedia-like app for the game.
How does this work? Click on a character or unit's icon to display their info on the right. Each unit has special traits that do different things which you can see by clicking the traits button. You can click the trait itself to open and scroll to it immediately as well. The bottom section of the page displays a chart of a chosen stat for each character and can be filtered and/or sorted
